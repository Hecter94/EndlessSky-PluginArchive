mission "CSSF Illumina Regions Attack"
	name "Defend <planet>"
	description "Defeat a CSSF raid on <destination>."
	minor
	repeat
	to offer
		"combat rating" > 100
		"random" > 70
	source
		not government "Pirate"
		attributes "illumina regions"
		government "Sayari Plushie"
	npc evade
		government "Yuyuko Saigyouji (Hostile)"
		personality heroic staying target harvests plunders
		fleet cssf.small 5
	npc
		government "Sayari Plushie"
		personality heroic staying
		fleet spa.small 1
		fleet spa.medium 1
		fleet spa.large 2
	on offer
		conversation
			`Suddenly you hear raised voices and shouting outside: "We are under attack! <planet> is under attack by the CSSF! We need every combat-worthy ship to join the defenses!" The authorities will probably pay you quite well if you assist them, but this could also be an easy way to get yourself killed.`
			choice
				`	(Stay here until the fight is over.)`
					decline
				`	(Join the defense fleet.)`
			`The Sayari Plushie Authority has deployed a small fleet to help repel the CSSF attackers, who can come in packs larger than the greatest pirates of human space. You join them, and take off together...`
				launch
	on visit
		dialog phrase "generic cssf fleet battle on visit"
	on complete
		payment 450000
		dialog phrase "generic cssf attack payment dialog"
