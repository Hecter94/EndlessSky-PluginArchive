mission "Midnight Incipias Arcus Expedition 1"
	minor
	name "Arcus Expedition"
	description "Escort the Arcus, a newly constructed transport-freighter to <system>."
	source "Pon'tes"
	destination "Turra"
	to offer
		has "event: midnight incipias first contact update"
	on offer
		conversation
			branch repeat
				has "Midnight Incipias Arcus Expedition 1: deferred"
			scene "thumbnail/nimbo cumulus-thumb"
			`You notice several new ships docked at the spaceport. As you look at them, an Incipias approaches you while flashing multiple colors. Clearly, it is trying to catch your attention.`
			label pursuit
			choice
				`	(Acknowledge its presence.)`
					goto converse
				`	(Try to evade it.)`
			`	You do your best to rush through the alien corridors of the station, but your feet cannot match the effortless gliding of your pursuer...`
				goto pursuit
			label repeat
			`As you walk through the spaceport you notice an Incipias following you while flashing multiple colors. Clearly, it is trying to catch your attention.`
				goto pursuit
			label converse
			`	The Incipias slows down as does the rate of its color fluctuations. Finally, the translator is able to interpret what it is trying to say. "Hello outer-traveler. Your assistance to our previous exploration vessel-ship has not been forgotten. We will be embarking on another voyage-expedition to <system>. Would you have any interest-curiosity to join us?"`
			choice
				`	"Yes, I would like to participate."`
					goto participate
				`	"What do you want me to do?"`
					goto explain
				`	I don't have time for this right now."`
				`	"No, I am not interested in this."`
					decline
			`	The Incipias glows blue. "I understand. Perhaps next time." If slowly drifts way, it's lights dim...`
				defer
			label explain
			`	The Incipias explains, "We will attempt a jump-shift to <system>. This time, we will use a vessel-ship that can carry enough crew and cargo to serve as the core for a colony-station."`
			choice
				`	"Looks like a good plan. I will participate."`
				`	"Sorry, I am not interested in this."`
					decline
			label participate
			scene "thumbnail/arcus-thumb"
			`	"Thank you. Our vessel-ship is a newly constructed transport-freighter: the Arcus. It is the first of it's kind-type. We hope it will not be the last."`
				accept
	npc save
		government "Hicemus"
		system "Dom'us"
		personality target waiting staying timid
		ship "Arcus FTL" "HIC Arcus #000001"
		on kill
			set "HIC Arcus #000001 destroyed"
	on enter "Hui'uc"
		fail

mission "Midnight Incipias Arcus Expedition 2"
	transition
	name "Arcus Expedition"
	description "Return the crew of the Arcus to <destination>."
	source
		system "Hui'uc"
	destination "Pon'tes"
	to offer
		has "Midnight Incipias Arcus Expedition 1: offered"
		not "HIC Arcus #000001 destroyed"
	on offer
		conversation
			scene "thumbnail/arcus derelict-thumb"
			`Soon after arriving, you notice the Arcus appears different. It seems to have aged from years, perhaps even decades, of neglect. It hails the <ship>. The signal is weak, but stable enough for the translator to convey a message. "Greetings, I presume you are the outer-helper our progenitors would light of..." The signal stutters.`
			``
			`	"... are not sure why, but after entering the space-between something surprise-unexpected occured. Our vessel-ship remained trapped there for two generation cycles. We used the supplies to sustain ourselves and our vessel-ship, but perhaps that may have only extended-prolonged the duration of our imprisonment..." The signal stutters again.`
			``
			`	"... eventually the power systems briefly falter-failed. To our surprise-unexpected, the vessel-ship was released once its systems were no longer operational. Outer-helper, we ask that you return us to <destination> on your vessel-ship. We no longer have enough supplies or crew to..." The signal failes completely. It appears you will have to board their ship if you want to help them.`
				accept
	npc board
		government "Derelict (Boardable)"
		system "Hui'uc"
		personality target waiting staying derelict mute
		ship "Arcus Derelict" "HIC Arcus #000001"
		dialog "The few remaining crew light their gratitude. From what you can see, much of the ship has been repurposed to prioritize long term survival over normal operations."
		on board
			set "HIC Arcus #000001 destroyed"
	on complete
		event "midnight add arcus" 30
		conversation
			scene "scene/scene-dom'us"
			`The Arcuses crew are grateful to finally see the home of their progenitors. While conversing with them during the journey, you learned that they are what humans would consider grandchildren of the original crew of the Arcus. The actual cause of their multi-generational entrapment is a mystery, but the research data they brought back should help Incipias scientists progress closer to achieving safe FTL travel.`

event "midnight add arcus"
	shipyard "Hicemus"
		add "Arcus"
	fleet "Incipias Standard Trade"
		add variant 1
			"Arcus"

It is our hope that you would be willing to assist if we encounter a surprise-unexpected occurence."`

				`	"I would be happy to help."`
					goto help
				`	"I am not able to help this time."`
				`	"I have no interest in helping."`
					decline
			`	The Incipias glows blue. "I understand. Perhaps next time." If slowly drifts way, it's lights dim...`
				defer
			label help
			`	The Incipias flashes several colors. "The crew will be pleased-excited to know that you will accompany them on this voyage-expedition. Thank you." It floats away, its lights bright.`

		system "Dom'us"
