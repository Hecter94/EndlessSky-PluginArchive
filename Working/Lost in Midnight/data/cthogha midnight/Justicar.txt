	landing
	invisible
	to offer
		has "Midnight Justicar First Contact 1: done"
	source Jurisprudence
	on offer
		conversation
			`You are escorted to your assigned landing pad. After disembarking, a trio of mechanical drones are waiting to escort you to the courthouse. As you walk, you notice that the city is only populated by robots or androids. Eventually, you arrive at a building that is difficult to distinguish from the others. The drones lead you to a kiosk. A robot situated on the other side addresses you.`
			`	"Captain <first> <last> identified. Priority incident 9173648520. Do you dispute your penalty?"`
			label guilty
			choice
				`	"I'm innocent!"`
					to display
						not "justicar guilty"
				`	"What crime am I guilty of?"`

			action
				set "justicar guilty"
			`	"Objection. Evidence of crime was detected. You are guilty."`
				goto guilty
			label crime
			`	"Justicar Code of Conduct Statute 3180 Subsection 512 paragraph 7."`
			choice
				`	"But what does that mean?"`
			scene "outfit/jump drive"
			`	"Summary: Use of an unapproved method of hyperspace travel. The standard penalty is a fine of 1,000,000 credits."`

				`	"So, about the that penalty..."`
			`	"Justicar Code of Conduct Statute 0066 Subsection 137 paragraph 11 allows an alternative penalty. The convict is placed into cryosleep for lifetime service in an Incarceration Cell, storing and providing energy as needed. Do you consent to the alternative penalty?"`
			choice
				`	"Yeah, no..."`
				`	"No, I'll pay the one million."`
					goto refuse
			scene "outfit/medium incarceration"
