mission "Inferno Dragon: Six Piece Clue"
	invisible
	minor
	source
		attributes "frontier"
		not attributes "pirate"
	to offer
		has "Inferno Dragon: Dragon's Wings: declined"
		has "Inferno Dragon: Dragon's Breath: declined"
		has "Inferno Dragon: Dragon's Claws: declined"
		or
			and
				not "Randomize Inferno Dragon: Six Piece Clue"
				random < 70
			and
				has "Randomize Inferno Dragon: Six Piece Clue"
				random < 10
	on offer
		conversation
			`While trying to relax at the bar, the mystery of the Inferno Dragon poem nags at you...`
			choice
				`	(Write down the lines you've gathered so far.)`
				`	(Forget it for now.)`
					defer
			`The Inferno Dragon's ambition reaches the heavens.`
			``
			`The Inferno Dragon's thirst can never be quenched.`
			``
			`The Inferno Dragon's presence causes all to tremble.`
			``
			`The Inferno Dragon's wings spread across the endless sky.`
			``
			`The Inferno Dragon's breath boils the boundless ocean.`
			``
			`The Inferno Dragon's claws ravage the timeless earth.`
			``
			`"That's an interesting poem," the waitress notices what you're writing.`
			choice
				`	"Do you know this poem?"`
				`	"It's a puzzle I'm trying to work out."`
					goto puzzle
			`"No, but I've dabbled in a bit of writing myself. I like the three themes represented here: sky, ocean, earth... But I think it would be clearer if the lines were rearranged so that each pair representing the themes were closer together."`
				goto end

			label puzzle
			`"A puzzle?" She looks closely at the lines of the poem. "I like the three themes represented here: sky, ocean, earth... But I think it would be clearer if the lines were rearranged so that each pair representing the themes were closer together."`
				goto end

			label end
			choice
				`	"Pairs?"`
				`	"How?"`
			action
				log "Inferno Dragon Notes: The six lines of the poem can be divided into three pairs. One pair related to the sky, one pair related to the ocean, and one pair related to the earth."
			`"Like this:"`
			``
			`	The Inferno Dragon's ambition reaches the heavens.`
			`	The Inferno Dragon's wings spread across the endless sky.`
			``
			`	The Inferno Dragon's thirst can never be quenched.`
			`	The Inferno Dragon's breath boils the boundless ocean.`
			``
			`	The Inferno Dragon's presence causes all to tremble.`
			`	The Inferno Dragon's claws ravage the timeless earth.`
			``
			`"Or something like that."`
			choice
				`	"That's interesting. Thanks."`
					decline
	on defer
		set "Randomize Inferno Dragon: Six Piece Clue"
