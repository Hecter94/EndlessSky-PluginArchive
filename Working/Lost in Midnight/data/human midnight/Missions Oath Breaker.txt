	minor
	name "Navy Patrol"
	description "A Navy Gunboat named R.N.S. Hongshui has been harassing our patrols at <waypoints>. Drive them off or capture the ship without killing the crew if possible. Destroy them only as a last resort. Payment is [<payment>]."
	"apparent payment" 150000
	source
		government "Free Worlds"
		near "Kornephoros" 1 3
		not attributes uninhabited
	waypoint Kornephoros
	to offer
		has "Liberate Kornephoros: done"
		not "event: fw armistice"
		"reputation: Republic" < 0
		random < 70
	on offer
		conversation
			scene "thumbnail/gunboat"
			`A militia officer approaches you. "Captain <first> <last>! The militia is trying to hunt down a Navy Gunboat named R.N.S. Hongshui that has been harassing our patrols at <waypoints>. Any assistance you can provide would be greatly appreciated. Our objective is to drive them off or capture the ship without killing the crew if possible. 				accept
	on waypoint
		log "People" "Captain Mensur" `Militia captain of the Korbschlaeger, an old Saber that is an unusual ship to find in the Militia. He has a deep hatred for the Republic.`
		conversation
			scene "thumbnail/saber"
			`You arrive at <waypoints> to find the R.N.S. Hongshui disabled and under heavy fire from a Saber named Korbschlaeger. This isn't the sort of ship normally used by militia pilots and captains...`
			choice
				`	(Hail the Korbschlaeger.)`
				`	(Hail the R.N.S. Hongshui.)`
					goto hongshui
				`	(Observe.)`
					goto observe
			`	There is no response.`
				goto observe
			label hongshui
			`	A panicked voice answers. "We surrender! Please stop your attack!"`
				goto observe
			label observe
			``
			scene "scene/scene-explosion"
			`	The Korbschlaeger continues it's brutal assault on the R.N.S. Hongshui showing no signs of relenting. Inevitably, a Javelin strike pushes the battered and broken Gunboat crew beyond any hope of salvation as it explodes in a ball of flames. The Korbschlaeger slowly turns towards you and sends a hail.`
			choice
				`	(Answer.)`
			scene "thumbnail/saber"
			`	The hail is audio only. A man's gruff voice addresses you. "Better luck next time. The reward for the Hongshui is ours."`
			choice
				`	"They were already trying to surrender!"`
				`	"You aren't like the other militia I've worked with."`
					goto ccor
			action
				fail "Midnight Korbschläger 1"
			`	The man doesn't hesitate to reply. "We are at war. I think you'll find that Republic scum won't be so merciful when you find yourself at their mercy. Captain Mensur signing off." The Korbschlaeger cuts the connection and leaves the system.`
				decline
			label ccor
			action
				fail "Midnight Korbschläger 1"
			`	The man laughs. "I signed up after the war started just for the opportunity to kill Republic scum. Make sure you stay out of my way if you don't want to get caught in the crossfire. Captain Mensur signing off." The Korbschlaeger cuts the connection and leaves the system.`
				decline
