	minor
	deadline 3
	repeat
	source "Scar's Hideout"
	to offer
		has "event: Ionic Leech North"
		random < 1
	on offer
		conversation
			branch infestation
				has "scar's hideout ionic leech attacked"
			`The lights suddenly dim. One of the pirates curses. "Damned Ionic Leeches!" Seeing your reaction, he explains, "A large enough swarm can drain the generators through the asteroid's outer crust. I'm sure once they've had their fill they'll leave."`
				goto end
			label infestation
			`The lights suddenly dim. There's probably another Ionic Leech infestation draining the power generators.`
			label end
			action
				set "scar's hideout ionic leech attacked"
			choice
				`	(Get rid of the Ionic Leech infestation.)`

				`	(Wait it out.)`
			`	After a few minutes, the lights return to normal.`
				defer
			label exterminate
			`	You decide to head out and exterminate the Ionic Leech swarm.`
				accept

		government "Indigenous Predator"
		personality waiting staying uninterested disables unconstrained mute ramming
		fleet "Large Ionic Leech"
		fleet "Small Ionic Leech" 2

				goto compensation
			
			label explain
			scene "outfit/hai tracker storage"
			`	"Geez, I didn't think I'd have to spell it out for you. I'm saying we're going to have to start attacking the Hai again if we don't get a fresh supply of Hai Trackers. Two hundred and eighty should keep us stocked for now."`

				`	"I'm not working for the Hai this time. How much are you willing to pay me?"`

			choice
				`	"Alright, I'll try to get you more Hai Trackers."`
