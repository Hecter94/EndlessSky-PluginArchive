mission "Ghost Hunting: Dire Omen 1"
	minor
	name "The Ghost Ship: Dire Omen"
	description "An old pirate told a tale of a ghost ship haunting the area near <system>. It might be interesting subject to ask the locals about if you ever visit <planet>, the pirate planet in that system."
	source
		planet "Trinket" "Greenrock" "Thule" "Smuggler's Den" "Albatross" "Bloodsea"
	destination "Stormhold"
	to offer
		"month" == 10
		"combat rating" > 512
		has "First Contact: Hai: declined"
		or
			has "main plot completed"
			and
				"year" >= 3015
				"month" >= 7
				"day" >= 4
		random < 70
	on offer
		conversation
			`You wander into one of several dimly lit, smoke-filled bars near your docking bay and notice a large crowd has gathered to listen as an old pirate tells a tale.`
			choice
				`	(Find a different bar.)`
				`	(Settle down and listen.)`
					goto listen
			
			`	You have no interest in old pirate tales and look elsewhere to relax.`
				defer
			
			label listen
			scene "planet/cloud1"
			`	"And so, afterwards me and the boys took our plunder. We then set off from Stormhold into the northern passage, where few dare to go unless ye have a good supply of fuel or a ramscoop. It was on the 3rd day of our voyage when it appeared out of the darkness: the Dire Omen. The ghost ship what haunts the northern passage descended upon us like a pelican on a bucket of sardines."`
			`	"A ghost ship!?!" you shout out. "Surely, you jest."`
			`	"It be real! I remember clear as day my tactical scanner readouts! That ship, the Dire Omen, has no crew! But it attacked us all the same. I escaped. The others... weren't so lucky."`
			`	The old pirate falls silent and stares somberly at his mug. The mood has shifted and it is clear he is done telling his tale.`
				accept
	on accept
		log `Heard an old pirate's tale of a ghost ship haunting the far north near <planet> in the <system> system. He claimed the ship had no crew. Probably just an embellished tale of being caught off guard by a stray Navy drone. Still, he told the tale well. It would make a good ghost story to frighten young children.`

mission "Ghost Hunting: Dire Omen 3"
	landing
	invisible
	source "Stormhold"
	to offer
		has "Ghost Hunting: Dire Omen 1: done"
	on offer
		conversation
			scene "planet/cloud1"
			`As you land on <planet>, you recall the tale told by an old pirate regarding a ghost ship haunting the northern passage. <planet> is the closest pirate stronghold to the southern entrance of that dark corridor through northern space. Do you want to ask the locals about the Dire Omen?`
			choice
				`	(Maybe another time.)`
				`	(I ain't afraid of no ghost!)`
					goto ghosthunt
			
			`You came here for other business. Ghost hunting will have to wait.`
				defer
			
			label ghosthunt
			`	While walking around the spaceport you notice a woman studying the sky with a telescope. She's dressed like a stereotypical cowgirl from an old Earth western. The pistol at her hip is an overt warning to not consider her an easy mark. It is a necessary fashion accessory on any pirate world.`
			`	"Looking for aliens?" you ask.`
			`	"Did y'all get that pickup line from some discount bin?" she replies with a drawl.`
			`	"Whoa, easy now." You raise your hands as a gesture of peace. "Actually, do you know anything about ghost ships?"`
			`	"The Dire Omen? Oh yeah, I may know a thang or two." She holds out her hand expectantly, palm up.`
			choice
				`	(Shake her hand.)`
				`	(Hand her 500 credits.)`
					goto tourist
				
			`"Uh, hello?" she says in irritation. "Info ain't free. Is this your first time on a pirate world?"`
			
			label tourist
				
			`	You begrudgingly hand over some chips worth 500 credits.`
			`	"Yee haw! I love tourists. We don't get 'em often 'round these parts, what with all the killin' and lootin'." After pocketing the chips she extends her hand again. This time it's an offer to shake hands. "I go by the name Calamity, but you can call me Cali!"`
			`	"I'm <first>. So, about the ghost ship?" you ask.`
			`	"Right. Right. So a while back this weird alien ship drifts into the system from out of the dark edges of human space. The old thang is powered down. Typical derelict. So a bunch of the 'ol pirate boys head on out to do some salvagin'. Ain't no one has ownership rights to a derelict 'cept the one who gets to it first, right? Right. Thang is, that ship weren't no derelict. Soon as some poor fool got close, the durn thang sprung into action like an angry bull with a hot poker jammed into its butt. The few who survived swear up and down that their tacticals showed the ship had no crew. Ever since then, that durn ghost ship's been hauntin' the northern passage."`
			`	"Can you tell me what the ship looked like?" you ask.`
			`	"They reckon it's shaped like a ring."`
				accept
	on accept
		payment -500
		log `Met someone on Stormhold named Cali who told me a story of how the ghost ship Dire Omen drifted into Alcyon from beyond human space. It might have spent centuries resting in peace before the pirates woke it up. Supposedly, it's shaped like a ring.`
		fail
		

mission "Ghost Hunting: Dire Omen 4"
	landing
	invisible
	source 
		planet "Farpoint" "Maelstrom" "Sunracer" "Skillet" "Prime"
	to offer
		has "Ghost Hunting: Dire Omen 3: failed"
		not "Ghost Hunting: Dire Omen 3: done"
	on offer
		conversation
			`As you start to land you notice smoke and flames coming from a nearby parked ship. That's not too unusual in this dangerous sector of space. What does catch your eye is the nature of the damage. Large portions of the hull look to have melted right off. Whatever did this must generate intense heat.`
			choice
				`	(Best not to get involved. Go find another place to land.)`
				`	(Stick around and find out what happened.)`
					goto investigate
			
			`	You hastily cancel the landing sequence for your autopilot and search for another landing pad.`
				defer
			
			label investigate
			`	When you exit your ship you hear the captain of the damaged ship filing a report with the port authority. "No, no. It wasn't pirates!" he exclaims. "It was the ghost ship. The Dire Omen." The government official stops typing on her datapad and gives the captain a skeptical look.`
			choice
				`	(Ignore them.)`
				`	(Ask him where he encountered the ghost ship.)`
					goto ask

			`	You shake your head at this superstitious nonsense and go about your business. This is probably some sort of insurance scam and from the expression on the face of the government official, she probably shares this sentiment.`
				decline
			
			label ask
			`	After he is done filing his report you approach the captain and ask, "Where did you last spot this 'ghost ship'?"`
			`	"About halfway through the Northern Passage," he responds. "I didn't believe the tales of a haunted war ship. But the legend of the 'Dire Omen' is real! According to my scanner, the ship has no crew."`
				accept
	on accept
		log `A merchant captain claimed to have been attacked by a ghost ship he referred to as the "Dire Omen". The government official who filed the report seemed skeptical of his testimony.`
		fail



mission "Ghost Hunting: Dire Omen 5"
	landing
	name "Rescue the 'Lucky Horse Shoe'"
	description "Merchants have asked you find their missing escort, the 'Lucky Horse Shoe', which was last seen heading towards Rajak in the northern passage. Supposedly, it was attacked by a ghost ship named 'Dire Omen'. If you manage to rescue it, escort the 'Lucky Horse Shoe' back to <destination>."
	source 
		planet "Farpoint" "Maelstrom" "Sunracer" "Skillet" "Prime"
	waypoint "Rajak"
	to offer
		or
			has "Ghost Hunting: Dire Omen 4: failed"
			has "Ghost Hunting: Dire Omen 4: declined"
	on offer
		conversation
			`As you open your hatch there is a group of merchants waiting for you. "Excuse us captain. We are seeking volunteers for a rescue mission. Will you hear us out?"`
			
			choice
				`	(Tell them to find someone else.)`
				`	(There's no harm in just listening to their request.)`
					goto listen
			
			`	"Sorry, I have my own business to attend to."`
				defer
			
			label listen
			`	"Our convoy was traveling through the northern passage when we were attacked by..." the merchant seems hesitant to continue.`
			`	"Pirates! We were attacked by pirates," another merchant interjects.`
			`	"No," says the first merchant. "We should speak the truth. You may find this hard to believe. But we were attacked by that ghost ship! The rumors are true!"`
			
			choice
				`	"What do you want from me?"`
				`	"You mentioned something about a rescue earlier..."`
				
			`	"Our escort, the 'Lucky Horse Shoe' was separated from us as we fled. We believe it is still out there. Would you be willing to search for it? We last saw it heading towards Rajak."`	
			
			choice
				`	(Tell them to find someone else.)`
				`	(Offer to help them.)`
					goto help
			
			`	"Forget it. I'm not going on a wild ghost chase."`
				decline
			
			label help
			`	"I'm willing to help, but..."`
			
			choice
				`	"You really believe that it was a ghost ship?"`
				`	"Will you be willing to pay me for my trouble?"`
					goto reward
						
			`	"It was like no other ship I'd ever seen before. And our tactical scanners showed no crew."`
				accept
				
			label reward
			`	"I'm certain the captain of the 'Lucky Horse Shoe' will repay you for your assistance."`
				accept

	on accept
		event "Dire Omen Clear Rajak"
		log `I've been asked to rescue the lost ship "Lucky Horse Shoe" which was last seen heading towards Rajak. Supposedly, it was escorting a merchant fleet when it was attacked by a ghost ship named "Dire Omen".`
	
	on enter "Rajak"
		conversation
			`When you enter the system your scanners show the "Lucky Horse Shoe" surrounded by some small pirate ships. Then something else arrives...`
			scene "scene/scene-dire omen"

	npc
		system "Rajak"
		government "Kor Mereti (Hostile)"
		personality target waiting staying disables harvests
		ship "Model 512 (dire omen)" "Dire Omen"

	npc
		system "Rajak"
		government "Pirate"
		personality waiting uninterested coward timid
		fleet "Small Northern Pirates" 3

	npc assist accompany save
		system "Rajak"
		government "Independent"
		personality derelict target timid
		ship "Mule (Hai Weapons)" "Lucky Horse Shoe"
		dialog `When you open the hatch you're surprised to be greeted by Cali, the person who sold you info back on Stormhold. "Well, ain't you a sight for sore eyes! Much obliged for your help, captain! I'll make sure to rustle up a reward for you once we get back to port."`
	
	on visit 
		dialog phrase "generic arrived-without-npc dialog"

	on abort
		event "Dire Omen Restore Rajak"

	on fail
		event "Dire Omen Restore Rajak"

	on complete

		log `The ghost ship "Dire Omen" turned out to be an alien ship with an advanced autopilot system. Like a drone. A very large and deadly drone.`
