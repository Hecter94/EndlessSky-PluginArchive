### kor.efret.shipyard
<br>
<br>
Adds a shipyard with the three Kor Efret ships to Laki Nemparu(Kashikt) in Kor Efret space. Also adds an outfitter with all outfits of these three ships and some Korath Exiles outfits. <br>
<br>
<br>
