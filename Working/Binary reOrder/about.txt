Adds a faction of AI that became fascinated with medieval Humans brought to them by the Pug. The humans are long gone, but their dreams of the sea live on.
This plugin exists primarily to make the ship sprites playable. Original names are mostly preserved, but liberties have been taken with ideas and balancing.
Intended tier: 1.5 - 2

Ship sprites by Todd1010
Loosely based on a plugin idea by Vaelophis Nyx
Other sprites borrowed from the base game
Questionable liberties taken by Kez
