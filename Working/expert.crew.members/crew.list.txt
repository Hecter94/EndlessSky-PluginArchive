 shields
  shields number
  "shield generation" number per frame. increases energy used
  "shield generation multiplier": multiplies the shield generation value of other outfits.
  "shield multiplier": multiplies the maximum shield value of the ship. (v. 0.10.3)
 hull
  hull: an additional number of hull points added to a ship's base hull value.
  "hull repair rate": the number of hull points regenerated per frame. It takes 1 energy to repair 1 unit of hull.
  "hull repair multiplier": multiplies the hull repair rate value of other outfits.
  "hull multiplier": multiplies the maximum hull value of the ship. (v. 0.10.3)
 energy
  "energy capacity": how much energy your ship can store.
  "energy generation": energy generated each frame. If you do not have any energy capacity, this energy does not carry over from frame to frame.
  "solar collection": the amount of energy that this outfit provides when your ship is 1250 pixels from the system center. 
 fuel
  ramscoop: fuel regeneration. Each frame, your ship gains fuel proportional to .03 * âˆš("ramscoop").
  "fuel capacity": the amount of fuel storage provided by this outfit.
  "fuel generation": fuel produced per frame. (v. 0.9.9)
 engine
  thrust: your ship's acceleration per frame equals thrust / mass, and your ship's max speed is thrust / drag.
  "reverse thrust": if your ship has reverse thrusters, the "back" button will apply reverse thrust instead of turning your ship around.
  turn: your ship's turn rate is (turn / mass) degrees per frame.
  "afterburner thrust": thrust produced each frame when using an afterburner.
 cooling
  cooling: heat subtracted from your ship by this outfit, per frame.
  "active cooling": amount of cooling this outfit provides when heat is at 100%, at which level it will consume its entire "cooling energy". 
  "heat capacity": adds to the ship's total mass where the ship's maximum heat capacity is concerned, but does not influence movement as mass 
 scanner
  "cargo scan power": the cargo scanning range is 100 times the square root of this number. you need four scanners to get twice the range 
  "cargo scan speed": the time it takes to complete a cargo scan is one second divided by the square root of this number. 
  "cargo scan efficiency": the maximum cargo scanning speed is the square root of this number. 
  "cargo scan opacity": increases the time required for other ships to perform a cargo scan on the ship with this attribute. 
  "outfit scan power": the outfit scanning range is 100 times the square root of this number. 
  "outfit scan speed": the time it takes to complete an outfit scan is one second divided by the square root of this number. 
  "outfit scan efficiency": the maximum outfit scanning speed is the square root of this number. 
  "outfit scan opacity": increases the time required for other ships to perform an outfit scan on the ship with this attribute. 
  "scan interference": your odds of a scan of your ship discovering anything illegal you have are equal to 1 / (1 + scan interference). 
  "scan concealment": prevents an equivalent mass of illegal goods from being scanned.
  "asteroid scan power": the asteroid scanning range is 100 times the square root of this number.
  "tactical scan power": the tactical scanning range is 100 times the square root of this number.
 storage
  bunks: additional crew / passenger space.
  "required crew": turrets (and maybe other high-end outfits) can increase your crew requirements.
  "outfit space": if negative, how much generic outfit space (as opposed to weapon or engine space) this outfit takes up.
  "engine capacity": if negative, how much engine space an outfit uses.
  "weapon capacity": set this to a negative value (generally, the same value as "outfit space") for any outfit that is a weapon. 
  "cargo space": amount of cargo space that this outfit adds (if positive) or removes (if negative) from your ship.
 other
  capture attack": this outfit can be wielded by a crew member and adds this amount to their strength of 1 when attacking
  "capture defense": this outfit can be wielded by a crew member and adds this amount to their strength of 2 when defending against boarders.
  cloak: how quickly a cloaking device cloaks or uncloaks.
  "landing speed": a value between 0 and 1, representing progress made per frame when landing or taking off.
