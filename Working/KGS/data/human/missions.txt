mission "KGS: More Gatling Guns"
	minor
	name "Retrieve Gatling Gun Tech"
	description "Barmy Edward has asked you to bring a Gatling Gun, a Twin Gatling, a Staggered Twin Gatling, and 1500 rounds of Gatling Gun ammo to <planet> for him to study."
	source "Skymoot"
	destination "Rust"
	to offer
		has "KGS: Twin Gatlings: declined"
	on offer
		conversation
			`As you explore <origin>'s spaceport, you encounter Barmy Edward, Chief Offensive Engineer of Kraz Cybernetics, and two children. "I'm on vacation," he says, "and who do I run into but Captain <last>? It's good seeing you!" You shake hands, and he introduces you to his two adopted daughters, Ludmina and Lunella. "Hello <first>!" they chorus in unison. Barmy looks at them affectionately. "Would you like to have dinner with us?"`
			choice
				` "Sure!"`
				` "I'm a bit busy, maybe not."`
					decline
			`	You are all waiting for dessert after a sumptious dinner, during which the two girls have been asking you nonstop about what being a spaceship captain is like. During a rare pause in the flow of questions, Barmy asks, "Have you seen any interesting weapons recently?"`
			choice
				` (Tell him about the Twin Gatlings you noticed at some pirate outfitters recently.)`
				` "No, not really."`
					goto no
			` "I have, actually." you say. "Do you know about the Gatling Guns that some pirates around these parts sell?" Barmy nods. "Some time ago I visited a pirate planet, and I saw what they called a Twin Gatling, two Gatlings shoved together to fit into a single gun port. What was interesting was that there was a more expensive version that supposedly fired alternately. It advertised cooldown time half as much as firing time, while the regular version fired like a normal Gatling - twice as much cooldown time as firing time.`
			` "Wow," says Barmy, "those pirates are quite creative. I have seen the Gatling Gun; it does incredible hull damage, but the downtime is significant. The staggered design must be much more effective. Actually, if I shoved three into a gun port, I could make them fire continuously! Imagine the hull ripping capability of that, plus our shield-focused Plasma Cannons. It would be an extremely effective combination! I would definitely like to do some research on these Gatlings - could you bring a few to my lab on Rust?"`
			choice
				` "Sure, what do you need?"`
					goto yes
				` "I'm sorry, but I don't think I can do that. You'll need to find someone else."`
			` Barmy looks slightly crestfallen. "Well," he says, "I'm sure I can find someone to get them."`
			label no
			` The stream of questions from Ludmina and Lunella renews. At last, dessert arrives, and you all dig in.`
			` After dessert, the conversation turns to the aliens you've seen, the planets you've visited. Eventually, they say their goodbyes and leave.`
				decline
			label yes
			` "Yay!" exclaims Barmy. "I think one copy of each Gatling, including the original, plus 1500 rounds of ammo for it would be sufficient." He gives you the address of his lab on Rust.`
			` The stream of questions from Ludmina and Lunella renews. At last, dessert arrives, and you all dig in.`
			` After dessert, the conversation turns to the aliens you've seen, the planets you've visited. Eventually, they say their goodbyes and leave.`
				accept
	on visit
		dialog `You have returned to <planet>, but you don't have the stuff Barmy requested. Go get them before returning.`
	on complete
		outfit "Gatling Gun" -1
		outfit "KGS Twin Gatling" -1
		outfit "KGS Staggered Twin Gatling" -1
		outfit "Gatling Gun Ammo" -1500
		payment 200000
		event "kgs: gatling mass production" 60 90
		conversation
			`You ask around for Barmy, and find that he's in. "Thank you, <first>," he says, handing you <payment>. "This will be very helpful. Keep an eye out for something in a few months."`
