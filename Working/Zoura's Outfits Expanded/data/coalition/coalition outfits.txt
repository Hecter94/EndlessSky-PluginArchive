# CLIP-ONS
# Special expansions installed on turret mounts.

outfit "Clip-On Outfit Pod"
	category "Systems"
	cost 100000
	thumbnail "outfit/outfit expansion"
	"mass" 40
	"drag" 3
	"turret mounts" -1
	"outfit space" 50
	"weapon capacity" -15
	"cooling inefficiency" 0.75
	"unplunderable" 1
	description `With nothing to use their ships' turret mounts on in peacetime, the species of the Coalition have each developed their own "clip-on" pods, using the turret floor as a base. The Saryds developed this versatile module as a way to expand outfit space, allowing their ships to install cargo expansions or extra bunk rooms to suit the task at hand.`
	description `	Each clip-on pod will significantly increase your ship's outfit space at the expense of its weapon capacity, and additionally the extra cooling loop required to accomodate the pod will interfere with your cooling systems. Additionally, as an external protrusion on the ship, clip-on pods will hamper the ship's maneuverability.`
