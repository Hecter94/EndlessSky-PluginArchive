mission "Echo Galleon Construction Shortage 1 - Scan"
	minor
	name "Echo-Galleon Scan"
	description "Find an Echo-Galleon that isn't moving around too much and scan its outfits. Deliver the scanner image to the shipyard on <destination>."
	source "Laki Nemparu"
	to offer
		has "event: Laki Nemparu opens Shipyard"
		random < 70
	on offer
		conversation
			scene "thumbnail/echo-galleon"
			`	Passing by the shipyard, you notice no Echo-Galleons available for sale. While shopping here shouldn't be much more difficult than simply pointing at the ship you want, trying to place an order for something that isn't in stock requires a bit more effort.`
			choice
				`	(I'm not interested in an Echo-Galleon.)`
				`	(Try to get a scan of an Echo-Galleon.)`
					goto scan
			scene "outfit/attention"
			`	The Echo-Galleon seems like a formidable ship. Are you sure that you aren't interested in ever buying one?`
			choice
				`	(I'm definitely not interested in buying an Echo-Galleon.)`
					decline
				`	(I'll try to get a scan of an Echo-Galleon.)`
			label scan
			scene "outfit/outfit scanner"
			`	Scanning an Echo-Galleon and bringing the image back to the shipyard might be the easiest way to show your interest in acquiring one. Cargo scanning is irrelevant but make sure you install an Outfit Scanner or something similar on your flagship.`
				accept
	on enter
		dialog "The <npc> appears to be in no rush to leave the system. That Echo-Galleon would be an ideal target for a scan."
	npc "scan outfits"
		government "Kor Efret"
		personality target waiting staying
		ship "Echo-Galleon" "Sesk Gratakfar"
		dialog "You've successfully scanned an Echo-Galleon. Return to <origin> with the data file."
	on fail
		dialog "[MISSION FAILED]: Wow, Echo-Galleons aren't so tough after all. If this were a game you could try reloading a save file to try again."
	on complete
		conversation
			scene "thumbnail/echo-galleon"
			`	After presenting the Kor Efret at the shipyard with an image of an Echo-Galleon, they respond by showing you images of various metals and minerals. Perhaps they simply don't have the resources to devote to building Echo-Galleons now.`
