mission "First Contact: Arlug"
	name "Got translation device. Oklen asked me to go to destination by <date>."
	description "Got translation device. Oklen asked me to go to destination by <date>."
	landing
	repeat
	source
		government Arlug
		not planet Destor
	destination Destor
	passengers 1
	on offer
		conversation
			`YAs you are coming down, you see a group of queer buildings. Maybe there are aliens on this planet. Do you want to explore them?`
			choice
				`	(no, take off again.)`
					goto leave
				`	(yes, why not?)`
					goto yes
			label leave
			`	As you are taking off, a warship jumps in, and attacks. The last thing you see is a queer looking beam come out off the ship and hit your escape pod. You die.`
				flee
			label yes
			`	You bring your ship down near it, and a security guard armed with a laser rifle. He halts you with the words, "Halt! The high-(he trails off into indecipherable sentences)-wants to see you." and beckons you to follow. You follow him inside a larger fancy  building, and the guard beckons you up the stairs and resumes his post outside. Do you want to go upstairs or downstairs?"`
					goto careful
			label careful
			choice
				`	(go upstairs)`
					goto Oklen
				`	(go downstairs)`
					goto "angry guard"
			label "angry guard"
			`	you slowly creep downstairs, and peer around the corner. There is no-one inside.`
			choice
				`	(Go slowly around the corner.)`
					goto "very angry guard"
				`	(Go back upstairs two stories where you where supposed to be in the first place.)`
					goto "Arlug distrust"
			label "very angry guard"
			`	you creep around the corner, but you trip over a box you hadn't noticed before, and make a loud crash. The guard comes downstairs "Halt!" He says, "You must be arrested, for violating a very important rule." He drags you off to jail, where you spend the rest of your life wishing that you had gone upstairs.`
				die
			label Oklen
			`	You step up the stairs, which are very steep. At the top you find a shrouded figure siting in a very nice carved chair. He says, "`
			choice
				`	"don't surprise me like that. it's scary! I'm leaving slowly and quietly."`
					goto angry
				`	(whisper "Yes, I will")`
					goto accept
			label accept
			`	"you take the translation device and exit.`
				accept
